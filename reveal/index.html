The Gameboy Camera and Printer

Some History

  • Released in 1998
  • Manufacturing ceased by 2003
  • Designed to be "light hearted entertainmet" for kids
  • Was compatble with almost 30 games

The Game boy camera was the first of its kind, and could perform several functions.

Features include:

  • Shoot: up to 30 photos, and subfeatures like panorama and selftimer
  • View: review images, create albums and even slideshows
  • Play: Game boy camera has built in mini games

The Game boy printer prints images from the camera, as well as from many Game boy games

Game boy printer features/functions:
  • A chip controls pulses of heat that create shades of grey on the thermal paper
  • Thermal paper is adhesive, and can be used as stickers
  • Nintendo provided third party access in order to expand the printer uses
  • Requires 6 AA batteries

What's interesting about Game boy Camera and Printer?

  • 1999 guinness world record for smallest digital camera
  • used to take the picture on the cover of Niel Young's Silver & Gold
  • considerd to be an early digital form of the selfie
